Data Privacy & Consent

Before proceeding, permission is required to access and analyze financial data.

1. Data Collection

Financial documents including bank statements, rent receipts, and utility bills are collected solely for credit assessment purposes.

2. AI Analysis

Automated artificial intelligence algorithms extract relevant information from submitted documents. Raw data is encrypted at rest.

3. Third-Party Sharing

Data is shared only with partnered lending institutions for loan processing and is never sold for marketing purposes.

4. Regulatory Compliance

The system adheres to financial regulations and data protection laws governing sensitive personal information.

5. Revocation

Consent may be revoked at any time by contacting support, resulting in deletion of processed data.
